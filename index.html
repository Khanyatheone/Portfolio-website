A Little About Me

My name is Khanya Michael-Angelo, i was born in Bloemfontein but spend my years in Johannesburg. I grew loving art and appreciating every essence it contains. The beginning of my primary years i enjoyed competing with my peers to determine who was better. Over the years my ability of art developed and i gradually started learning how to do grafitti " ". In the year of 2014 i competed in an art prize competition, requiring us to draw a zulu personal with a traditional touch to it.

In the year in 2015 i attended Allen Glen Highschool. Shortly After the year in 2017 i moved to Trinityhouse Randparkridge. I'm a capable student that is self-motivated and well orginised individual. I give my best to achive great results to achieve my goals. I'm an idependent thinker and can easily share ideas and opinions on arange of subjects without offending anyone. I am kind, polite and quite but capable of holding my own in any conversation or class discussions, but still a social being. I enjoy working idependent , being able to think creatively and pay attention to detail, well prepared for tasks when given. I have a ability to self reflect deeply and always willing to help people around. The website is my starting point to not only showcase my poetential in art but to psoitively bring light to people who have depression, abuse, gender based violence and anything that has an affect on mental health. I'm very mindful individual taking others persepctive into consideration.
